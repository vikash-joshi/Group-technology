About Us

Home  /  About Us

Group Technology is a leading company specializing in the installation, system integration, and maintenance of modern security and communication systems. With over a decade of industry experience, the company has steadily evolved alongside technological advancements to meet the changing needs of its clients.

We proudly serve a diverse clientele that includes star hotels and resorts, corporate offices, housing societies, real estate developers, temple complexes, NGOs, educational institutions, and banks. Our operations span across Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, Thane, Dombivli, and Kalyan.

With our state-of-the-art infrastructure in the bustling Tardeo area of Mumbai, Group Technology continues to provide reliable and innovative solutions for modern security requirements.
